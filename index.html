Visit Skardu

Skardu is a scenic town located in the Gilgit-Baltistan region of Pakistan.

Three most beautifull places in Skardu you must visit

Shangrila Resort

Shangrila Resort, also known as "Heaven on Earth," is located in the heart of Skardu Valley. This resort is surrounded by lush green gardens, fruit orchards, and a beautiful lake. The resort offers visitors a chance to relax and take in the serene natural beauty of Skardu.

Upper Kachura Lake

Upper Kachura Lake is a picturesque alpine lake located about 20 minutes from Skardu. The crystal-clear blue waters of the lake are surrounded by towering snow-capped mountains and provide a stunning backdrop for photography enthusiasts.

Satpara Lake

Satpara Lake is one of the largest freshwater lakes in Pakistan, located about 9 kilometers from Skardu. The lake is nestled amidst snow-capped mountains and surrounded by green pastures. Visitors can enjoy boating and fishing in the lake, as well as take in the stunning views of the surrounding landscapes.
